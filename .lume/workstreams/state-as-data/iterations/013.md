# Iteration 013 - P11: Convert iterations — iterations/NNN.json as source

## DoD

Replace `iterations/NNN.md` as the authored source for iteration content with a
schema-validated `iterations/NNN.json`. NNN.md becomes a derived view,
regenerated by the engine on every mutation. No machine field is read from
NNN.md after this change.

Content schema (`iteration_content`): `{id, dod: {preamble, items[]}, self_review, handback}`.
Verdict stamps remain in `state.json` (done in P5); this change captures
the prose content.

Changes confined to `iteration.py`, `workstream.py`, and `migrate.py`.

      handback, verdict stamps from entity.verdicts).

      `iteration_content` schema) with DoD skeleton seeded from type-specific

      NNN.md immediately regenerated as a view.
      (Evidence: after `lume open`, NNN.json exists and validates; NNN.md
      contains the skeleton items.)
